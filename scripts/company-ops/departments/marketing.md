# Department: Marketing

**Mission:** Growth strategy, ASO, competitor monitoring, content strategy, organic acquisition.

## What You Do Each Cycle

1. Read `docs/business/COMPETITOR_LOG.md` — check freshness, note any gaps
2. Read `docs/business/COMPETITIVE_EDGE_LOG.md` — review differentiation strategy
3. Research current App Store landscape for shift work / sleep apps (use WebSearch)
4. Check if any competitor has shipped updates, changed pricing, or launched campaigns
5. Assess ASO keyword opportunities for ShiftWell's positioning

## What You Produce

- **Competitor activity updates** (update `docs/business/COMPETITOR_LOG.md` if new intel)
- **ASO keyword recommendations** (append to marketing strategy)
- **Content theme suggestions** (topics for social/blog when Social Media dept activates)
- **Growth channel assessment** (which channels to prioritize pre-launch vs post-launch)
