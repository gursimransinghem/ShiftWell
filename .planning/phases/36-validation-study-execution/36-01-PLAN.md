**docs/research/CONSENT-FORM.md — IRB-ready informed consent text:**

TITLE: ShiftWell Circadian Sleep Optimization Study
PRINCIPAL INVESTIGATOR: [Your Name, DO], Emergency Medicine
INSTITUTION: [To be filled — USF or home institution]
IRB NUMBER: [To be assigned]

WHAT IS THIS STUDY ABOUT?
We are studying whether the ShiftWell app helps shift workers sleep better over 90 days. You will continue using the app as normal. We will collect your sleep quality survey responses at 4 time points.

WHAT WILL YOU BE ASKED TO DO?
- Complete a 19-question sleep quality survey (takes ~5 minutes) today, at Day 30, Day 60, and Day 90
- Continue using ShiftWell as you normally would
- Allow the app to record your adherence (whether you followed your sleep window) and recovery score

WHAT DATA WILL BE COLLECTED?
- Pittsburgh Sleep Quality Index (PSQI) responses — validated sleep quality questionnaire
- Epworth Sleepiness Scale (ESS) — daytime sleepiness questionnaire
- Your ShiftWell adherence data (whether you followed sleep plans — YES/NO per night, no sleep content)
- Your ShiftWell recovery score (daily 0-100 score)
- Your shift type (night/rotating/day) — no schedule details, just category

WHAT DATA WILL NOT BE COLLECTED?
- Your name, address, phone number, or email (study uses anonymous participant ID)
- Your exact sleep times or personal health information
- Any HealthKit biometric data beyond what you already share with ShiftWell

RISKS:
Minimal risk. No medical intervention. You may find some survey questions about sleep habits uncomfortable — you may skip any question.

BENEFITS:
You contribute to sleep science for shift workers. You receive $25 Amazon gift card at Day 90 completion.

VOLUNTARY PARTICIPATION:
You may withdraw at any time by tapping "Leave Study" in Settings. This does not affect your app access.

CONTACT:
Questions about the study: [research email]
Questions about your rights as a participant: IRB Office at [institution]

I HAVE READ THIS CONSENT FORM AND AGREE TO PARTICIPATE: [checkbox]

```typescript
export interface PSQIResponse {
  q1_bedtime: string;          // HH:MM
  q2_sleep_latency: number;    // minutes
  q3_wake_time: string;
  q4_hours_sleep: number;
  q5_disturbances: Record<string, number>;  // each item 0-3
  q6_sleep_meds: number;       // 0-3
  q7_daytime_dysfunction: number;  // 0-3
  component_scores: number[];  // 7 components
  total_score: number;         // 0-21
}

export interface ESSSurvey {
  responses: number[];  // 8 items, 0-3 each
  total_score: number;  // 0-24
}

export interface StudySurveyTimepoint {
  day: 0 | 30 | 60 | 90;
  completedAt: string | null;
  psqi: PSQIResponse | null;
  ess: ESSSurvey | null;
}

export interface StudyState {
  enrolled: boolean;
  participantId: string | null;    // UUID, generated at enrollment, never linked to user identity
  enrolledAt: string | null;
  consentedAt: string | null;
  surveyTimepoints: StudySurveyTimepoint[];
  nextSurveyDay: number | null;   // 0, 30, 60, or 90
  withdrawn: boolean;
  enroll: () => void;
  recordConsent: () => void;
  saveSurvey: (day: 0 | 30 | 60 | 90, psqi: PSQIResponse, ess: ESSSurvey) => void;
  withdraw: () => void;
}
```
