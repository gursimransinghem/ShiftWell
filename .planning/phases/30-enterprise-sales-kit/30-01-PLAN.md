**docs/enterprise/ROI-CALCULATOR.md:**

A step-by-step worked example an employer can follow with their own numbers:

```
STEP 1 — Enter your numbers:
  Nursing headcount: ___
  Average annual salary: $___
  Current unplanned call-out rate (shifts/person/year): ___
  Current annualized turnover rate: ____%

STEP 2 — Apply conservative estimates (ShiftWell assumptions):
  Call-out reduction: 15%
  Turnover reduction: 10%
  Presenteeism improvement: 10% of $1,967/employee/year (RAND 2016)

STEP 3 — Compute:
  Reduced call-out cost = headcount × call_out_rate × 0.15 × $3,600 (NIOSH)
  Reduced turnover cost = headcount × turnover_rate × 0.10 × salary × 0.5 (SHRM)
  Reduced presenteeism = headcount × $196.70
  Total annual savings = sum of above

STEP 4 — Subtract platform cost:
  Platform cost = headcount × $10/seat × 12 months
  Net ROI = (total_savings - platform_cost) / platform_cost × 100%

WORKED EXAMPLE (100-nurse ICU):
  Headcount: 100 | Salary: $75,000 | Call-outs: 8/yr | Turnover: 27%
  Reduced call-out cost: 100 × 8 × 0.15 × $3,600 = $43,200
  Reduced turnover cost: 100 × 0.27 × 0.10 × $75,000 × 0.50 = $101,250
  Reduced presenteeism: 100 × $196.70 = $19,670
  Total savings: $164,120
  Platform cost: 100 × $10 × 12 = $12,000
  Net ROI: ($164,120 - $12,000) / $12,000 = 1,268%
```

**docs/enterprise/COMPLIANCE-DOCS.md:**

Section 1 — HIPAA Readiness Summary (for prospects)
- ShiftWell acts as a Business Associate (BA) under HIPAA when deployed by covered entities
- Business Associate Agreement (BAA): available as standard contract term, included in Enterprise tier
- De-identification: all cohort metrics de-identified per HIPAA Safe Harbor (45 CFR §164.514(b))
- Differential privacy: applied for cohorts < 50 to prevent re-identification
- Data residency: all PHI stored on US-East AWS infrastructure
- Encryption: AES-256 at rest, TLS 1.3 in transit
- Access control: employer sees only their cohort data, never another employer's data
- Audit logs: all PHI access logged (timestamp, user, action)
- Breach notification: within 60 days per HIPAA Breach Notification Rule

Section 2 — BAA Template Outline
Key terms ShiftWell's BAA will include:
- ShiftWell as Business Associate, health system as Covered Entity
- Permitted uses: providing enterprise dashboard and outcome analytics
- Safeguards: Technical, administrative, and physical safeguards per HIPAA §164.312
- Breach notification: ShiftWell notifies CE within 10 business days of discovery
- Subcontractors: AWS (BAA in place), no other subcontractors with PHI access
- Termination: PHI destroyed or returned within 30 days of contract termination
[Note: Full BAA requires attorney review — this is a term outline, not legal advice]
