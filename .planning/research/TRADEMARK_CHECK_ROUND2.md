## Top Picks (Lowest Risk, Best Brandability)

1. **Circawell** -- cleanest slate across all dimensions

## Detailed Findings

### 1. Restwell

**App Store:**
- **DIRECT CONFLICT.** RestWell is an active, well-established sleep app at restwell.app with a 4.9-star App Store rating and 50K+ reviews. It offers sleep tracking, insights, and habit-building tools. Claims 89% of users feel more rested after 30 days. This is a direct competitor in the exact same space.
- Additionally: "SleepWell" and "Sleep Well" variants are also taken by multiple apps.

**Trademark/Brand:**
- Restwell Sleep Products is a Canadian mattress manufacturer (est. 1990) at restwell.com, making Spring Air and other brands. Active, established company.
- The RestWell app is a separate entity operating at restwell.app.
- Between these two, the "Restwell" brand space in sleep/wellness is heavily occupied.

**Domains:**
- restwell.com -- TAKEN (mattress manufacturer)
- restwell.app -- TAKEN (sleep app)
- restwell.io -- Unknown, likely available

**Risk Rating: HIGH** -- Direct conflict with an active, popular sleep app. Non-starter.

**Brandability: 7/10** -- Sounds great, but unusable due to conflicts.

---

### 2. Slumber

**App Store:**
- **DIRECT CONFLICT.** Slumber by Slumber Studios is a major, well-funded sleep app with 18M+ downloads across apps. Featured by Apple, Google, NYT, WSJ, and Washington Post. Offers sleep stories, meditations, soundscapes. 4.3 stars on Google Play with 100K+ downloads on Android alone. 400K+ five-star reviews across all apps.
- Slumber Studios is a real company (Durham, NC, 11-50 employees) with multiple apps including "Deep Sleep Sounds."

**Trademark/Brand:**
- Slumber Studios operates slumberstudios.com and almost certainly holds trademarks in Class 9/42/44.
- The brand "Slumber" is deeply established in the sleep app market.

**Domains:**
- slumber.com -- Likely controlled by Slumber Studios
- slumber.app -- Unknown but likely taken or expensive
- slumber.io -- Unknown

**Risk Rating: HIGH** -- One of the most established names in sleep apps. Absolute non-starter.

**Brandability: 8/10** -- Beautiful word, but completely unavailable.

---

### 3. SleepPilot

**App Store:**
- **DIRECT CONFLICT.** "Sleep Pilot - Sleep Tracker" is an active app by Sleep Pilot Technology Co., Ltd. (Chengdu, China, founded 2017). Available on both iOS and Android. Tracks sleep via microphone analysis -- snoring, sleep talking, body movement. Subscription at $29.99/year. Updated for iOS 26 with Liquid Glass interface.

**Trademark/Brand:**
- Sleep Pilot Technology Co., Ltd. owns sleeppilot.com and operates the app.
- "Anti Sleep Pilot" is another related brand (Copenhagen-based).

**Domains:**
- sleeppilot.com -- TAKEN (by the app company)
- sleeppilot.app -- Unknown
- sleeppilot.io -- Unknown

**Risk Rating: HIGH** -- Direct name collision with an active sleep tracker app. Non-starter.

**Brandability: 6/10** -- Descriptive and functional but lacks distinctiveness. "Pilot" is overused in tech.

---

### 4. Circadian (standalone)

**App Store:**
- **DIRECT CONFLICT.** "Circadian: Your Natural Rhythm" is an active app with 140K+ downloads, 800+ ratings (4.15 stars on Android). Built on Nobel Prize-winning chronobiology. Offers bedtime calculator, biorhythm tracking, circadian alarm.
- Additionally: MyCircadianApp, myCircadianClock, AYO Circadian Health, Peaks: Circadian Rhythm, Lume: Circadian Health, and a "Circadian Health Bundle" all exist on the App Store.

**Trademark/Brand:**
- "Circadian" is a generic/descriptive scientific term (circadian rhythm) which makes it weak as a trademark but also means the App Store is flooded with apps using this word.
- Multiple companies use "Circadian" in their name: Circadian Technologies (consulting), Circadian Health (virtual care), Circadian Wellness (supplements), Circadia (skincare).

**Domains:**
- circadian.com -- TAKEN
- circadian.life -- TAKEN (app)
- circadian.app -- Unknown but likely taken

**Risk Rating: HIGH** -- Too generic, too crowded. Weak trademark potential for a standalone word that describes the very science your app is built on.

**Brandability: 5/10** -- Scientifically accurate but zero distinctiveness. Impossible to own in search or App Store.

---

### 5. Circadio

**App Store:**
- No iOS or Android sleep/health apps found with this name.
- A defunct Mac desktop time-tracking app called "Circadio" existed circa 2013 for the "5pm" project management tool. Appears completely abandoned.

**Trademark/Brand:**
- A Tracxn profile for "Circadio" exists but describes it as an unfunded UK company providing "foraging courses, ecological consulting, and herbal medicine services" -- completely different industry and appears inactive/minimal.
- No USPTO registrations found in search results.
- Circadia (skincare) is a related-sounding brand but different enough.

**Domains:**
- circadio.com -- Possibly parked or owned by the defunct time tracker / UK company. Needs direct WHOIS check.
- circadio.app -- Likely available
- circadio.io -- Likely available

**Risk Rating: LOW-MEDIUM** -- Minor legacy conflicts from abandoned products in unrelated categories. The Circadia skincare brand is the closest concern phonetically but different enough (and different industry).

**Brandability: 7/10** -- Strong sound. "Circa" (around/cycle) + "dio" gives it a modern, app-friendly feel. Evokes circadian rhythm without being generic. Easy to pronounce. Distinctive enough to own in search. One concern: some people might confuse it with Circadia.

### 6. Circawell

**App Store:**
- **NO CONFLICTS FOUND.** Zero results for any app named "Circawell" on iOS or Android.

**Trademark/Brand:**
- **NO CONFLICTS FOUND.** No companies, products, or trademark registrations surfaced in any search.
- Complete white space.

**Domains:**
- circawell.com -- Likely available (no web presence found)
- circawell.app -- Likely available
- circawell.io -- Likely available

**Risk Rating: LOW** -- Cleanest name in the entire list. No conflicts in any category.

**Brandability: 7/10** -- Clearly communicates circadian + wellness. Easy to spell and pronounce. Modern compound-word construction (cf. Headwell, Livewell). Professional/medical feel appropriate for a health app. Slightly less "fun" than Circadio but more immediately meaningful. The "well" suffix directly signals wellness/health category.

### 7. Circanova

**App Store:**
- No direct conflicts for "Circanova" specifically.

**Trademark/Brand:**
- **Ciranova** (one letter different) is an established wood finishing/coatings brand by Debal Coatings / Blanchon Group with 80+ years of history. Operates ciranovastore.com. This is a real trademark concern.
- **CircNova, Inc.** is a VC-funded biotech company ($3.3M raised) working on circular RNA therapeutics. Founded 2023. Featured in TechCrunch.
- **Circana** is a massive market research firm (7,000+ brands use it).
- The "Circa-" + "-nova" space is crowded with established brands, even though none are in sleep/health apps.

**Domains:**
- circanova.com -- Likely taken or conflicting with Ciranova
- circanova.app -- Possibly available
- circanova.io -- Possibly available

**Risk Rating: MEDIUM** -- No direct app conflict, but phonetic/spelling proximity to Ciranova (coatings) and CircNova (biotech, funded) creates confusion risk. A trademark examiner might flag similarity to Ciranova.

**Brandability: 6/10** -- "Nova" (new) is overused in tech branding. The Ciranova confusion is a real problem. Sounds more like a biotech or space company than a sleep app.

---

### 8. Circaline

**App Store:**
- **NO CONFLICTS FOUND.** No sleep or health apps with this name.

**Trademark/Brand:**
- circaline.com is registered to a consulting firm ("We lead change in the way organizations operate to achieve their objectives"). Appears to be a small/niche business consulting company.
- "Circline" (without the 'a') is a well-known type of fluorescent light bulb. Could create odd associations.
- An Anthropologie fashion item called "CircALine" was found (elephant strapless dress).
- No USPTO registrations found in the app/software space.

**Domains:**
- circaline.com -- TAKEN (consulting firm)
- circaline.app -- Likely available
- circaline.io -- Likely available

**Risk Rating: LOW-MEDIUM** -- The consulting firm at circaline.com is a minor concern but in a completely different industry. The fluorescent bulb association ("circline") is an odd semantic neighbor.

**Brandability: 5/10** -- Less intuitive meaning than Circawell or Circadio. "Line" doesn't clearly connect to sleep/wellness. The circline/fluorescent bulb association is unfortunate. Sounds more like a transit/transportation brand. Not as memorable or distinctive.

---

### 9. Circadian Well

**App Store:**
- No exact match found for "Circadian Well" as a two-word app name.
- However, the entire "Circadian ___" namespace is crowded (see #4 above).

**Trademark/Brand:**
- **Circadian Wellness Corp** exists (Lakeway, TX, founded 2020). Sells supplements for sleep, cognition, energy, libido, and pain relief using "Quicksome" solubilizing technology. This is a direct naming conflict in the wellness/sleep space.
- Multiple other "Circadian + [word]" brands exist: Circadian Health, Circadian Technologies, etc.
- Two-word names are harder to trademark when both words are generic/descriptive.

**Domains:**
- circadianwell.com -- Unknown, needs WHOIS check
- circadianwell.app -- Unknown

**Risk Rating: MEDIUM** -- Circadian Wellness is too close in both name and category (sleep supplements vs. sleep app). A trademark examiner would likely flag this. The two-word format also makes it less distinctive as a brand and harder to use as a URL slug / social handle.

**Brandability: 6/10** -- Immediately communicates the concept but feels descriptive rather than branded. Two-word app names feel dated (modern apps trend toward single coined words). "Circadian Wellness" already exists, making this feel derivative. Hard to turn into a clean social handle (@circadianwell is long).

---

## Recommended Next Steps

1. **Run a formal WHOIS check** on circawell.com/.app/.io and circadio.com/.app/.io to confirm domain availability
2. **File a direct USPTO TESS search** (tmsearch.uspto.gov) for "Circawell" and "Circadio" to confirm zero registrations
3. **Secure domains immediately** for whichever name is chosen -- these coined words could be snapped up
4. **Consider Circawell as the frontrunner** -- it has the cleanest IP landscape and directly communicates the app's value proposition (circadian + wellness)
5. **Circadio as strong backup** -- slightly more memorable/fun but has minor legacy noise
