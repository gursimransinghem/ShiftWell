> **Purpose:** Evaluate two name candidates for ShiftWell rebrand
> **Methodology:** Web search across App Store, USPTO/Trademarkia, domain landscape, Google brand presence, international brands

## 1. CIRCU (pronounced "seer-KOO")

### App Store Search
- **No exact "Circu" app found on iOS App Store** as a standalone listing
- **Circu App (circuapp.com)** — A social networking app ("Find Your People") that helps users join interest-based groups and local events. Has its own website at circuapp.com. Appears to be a small/early-stage social platform, not widely downloaded
- **Nearby names:** Circ (Circ Marketplace — sustainable fashion), Circa (location-based college social app), Circul+ (BodiMetrics health ring app — blood oxygen, sleep, BP), Circoo (messaging app)
- **CIRCUL+ is the closest health competitor** — it is a ring-based health tracker measuring sleep, SpO2, BP, and HR. Uses circu.net-adjacent naming

### USPTO Trademark
- No direct "CIRCU" trademark found in software/health classes via web search
- The Circu Magical Furniture brand (Portuguese luxury children's furniture, Covet Group) holds the "Circu" name commercially — operates at **circu.net**
- Circu App (social platform) operates at circuapp.com — unclear if trademarked

### Google Presence
- **Circu Magical Furniture dominates search results** — Portuguese luxury children's furniture brand (beds shaped like rockets, hot air balloons). Part of Covet Group. Established 2014. High-end, well-known in design circles
- Circu App (social platform) has some presence but minimal
- e-circu.com flagged by some security checkers (unrelated)

### International Conflicts
- **Circu Magical Furniture (Portugal)** — Major conflict. Established brand with international retail presence (NYC, Paris, Porto). Sells through 1stDibs, luxury retailers. Well-indexed globally. However, they are in Class 20 (furniture), not Class 9/42/44 (software/health)
- Cross-class risk is LOW but brand confusion in Google search is HIGH

### Risk Rating: **MEDIUM-HIGH**
### Brandability Score: **6/10**

**Pros:**
- Elegant, French-inspired pronunciation
- Strong circadian association
- No exact match in health/sleep app space
- Distinctive and memorable

**Cons:**
- Circu Magical Furniture dominates Google results — SEO will be an uphill battle
- circuapp.com is already taken by a social app called "Circu"
- CIRCUL+ health ring app is confusingly similar in the health wearable space
- circu.net taken, circu.com likely unavailable
- Pronunciation ambiguity (sir-koo? sir-kyoo? kir-koo?)

## 2. RHYTHM (standalone single word)

- Dozens of "Rhythm + modifier" apps exist: Rhythm Lab, Rhythm Generator, Rhythm Training, Rhythm Learning, Rhythm Lighting, Complete Rhythm Trainer, Heart Rhythm 2026
- Most are music education apps — not health/sleep
- **Rhythm Lighting** (Philips Hue circadian lighting) is health-adjacent

### Risk Rating: **HIGH**

## 3. VARIATIONS

### Circu (with accent)

- Same conflicts as Circu above
- Accent marks create UX friction (hard to type on English keyboards, URL encoding issues)
- The furniture brand already uses the unaccented version
- **Risk: MEDIUM-HIGH | Brandability: 5/10**
- Accent does not provide meaningful trademark differentiation

### Rhythmwell

- **No existing app or brand found** with this exact name
- rhythm-well.com is used by **Rhythm Wellness** — an insomnia treatment practice in Houston offering CBT-I
- rhythmwell.in exists — a health/wellness site in India
- The compound word is unique enough to potentially register
- Decent SEO differentiation from "Rhythm" alone
- Still inherits some "Rhythm" trademark adjacency risk
- **Risk: MEDIUM | Brandability: 6/10**
- Feels slightly generic/corporate, less punchy

### Rhythm Sleep

- **No exact app found** with this name
- Extremely generic descriptor combination — likely not registrable as a trademark
- "Rhythm" + "Sleep" are both common dictionary words in the health space
- Would be nearly impossible to defend legally
- SEO would be buried under Sleep Cycle, Circadian apps, etc.
- **Risk: HIGH | Brandability: 2/10**
- Functionally unusable as a brand name

### Circu Sleep

- **No existing app or brand found** with this exact name
- circusleep.com appears available
- Combines the Circu name with clear category signal
- Less elegant than "Circu" alone but more defensible
- Still carries the Circu Magical Furniture proximity issue
- **Risk: MEDIUM | Brandability: 5/10**
- The "Sleep" suffix makes it feel like a product line, not a standalone brand

## Recommendation

**Neither "Circu" nor "Rhythm" is a clean winner.**

- **Rhythm** should be eliminated — the trademark landscape is too crowded with well-funded health companies (two public companies on NASDAQ), and domain availability is zero.
- **Circu** is more viable but carries real risks: the Portuguese furniture brand dominates Google, and the social app already owns circuapp.com. The name would require creative domain work (circuhealth.com, circuwell.com, trycircu.com, etc.) and a willingness to compete for SEO visibility.
- **Rhythmwell** is the sleeper pick from the variations — unique enough to potentially register, no direct app conflicts found, but feels slightly generic.

**If forced to choose between the two primaries:** Circu > Rhythm, by a significant margin. But a Round 4 of naming exploration may be warranted.
