# ShiftWell Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** March 1, 2026
**Last Updated:** March 14, 2026

## Introduction

ShiftWell ("the App") is a sleep optimization app designed for shift workers. It is developed and maintained by **Dr. Gursimran Singh** ("we," "us," or "our").

We built ShiftWell with privacy as a core principle. Your data stays on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your information on external servers. This privacy policy explains what data the App uses, how it is handled, and what rights you have.

---

## What Data Does ShiftWell Use?

ShiftWell stores the following information **locally on your device only**:

- **Shift schedule** — Your work shift dates and times, including schedules imported from .ics calendar files
- **Chronotype quiz answers** — Your responses to the in-app chronotype assessment
- **Sleep preferences** — Your preferred sleep and wake times, nap preferences, and related settings
- **Household profile** — General household information you provide (such as family size, whether you have children or pets) used to tailor sleep recommendations

This data is stored using on-device storage (AsyncStorage) and **never leaves your device**.

---

## What Data Do We Collect?

**None.** We do not collect, transmit, receive, or have access to any of your data.

- No account creation or login is required.
- No data is sent to our servers or any third-party servers.
- No analytics or usage tracking is performed.
- No advertising networks or ad-related SDKs are included.
- No third-party SDKs that collect user data are included in the App.

---

## Calendar File Import and Export

ShiftWell allows you to:

- **Import** .ics calendar files from your device to load your shift schedule
- **Export** .ics calendar files containing your optimized sleep schedule

These operations happen entirely on your device using standard iOS file-handling capabilities. We do not access, read, or store any files beyond what you explicitly select within the App.

---

## How Is Your Data Stored and Protected?

All data is stored locally on your device using standard on-device storage. Your data is protected by your device's built-in security features, including device passcode, Face ID, or Touch ID.

Because no data is transmitted from your device, there is no risk of server-side data breaches involving your ShiftWell data.

---

## Data Retention and Deletion

Your data remains on your device for as long as you use the App.

- **Delete your data at any time** by going to **Settings > Clear All Data** within the App.
- **Uninstalling the App** will also remove all stored data from your device.

We do not retain any copies of your data because we never have access to it in the first place.

---

## Future Features

We may introduce optional features in future versions of the App that involve additional data access, such as:

- **Apple HealthKit integration** — With your explicit permission, the App may read or write sleep-related data from Apple Health (such as sleep duration or bedtime schedules). HealthKit data access will always require your opt-in consent and can be revoked at any time through your device's Settings. HealthKit data will not be sold, shared with third parties, or used for advertising.
- **Cloud sync or account features** — If introduced, these will be optional and clearly disclosed. This privacy policy will be updated before any such features are released.

Any future changes that affect how your data is handled will be reflected in an updated version of this privacy policy with a new effective date.

If you believe a child under 13 is using the App in a way that concerns you, please contact us at the email below.

## Your Rights Under GDPR (European Users)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), including the right to access, correct, delete, and port your personal data.

Because ShiftWell stores all data locally on your device and we have no access to it:

- **Access and portability:** Your data is already entirely in your possession on your device. You can export your sleep schedule as an .ics file at any time.
- **Deletion:** You can delete all App data through Settings > Clear All Data, or by uninstalling the App.
- **Correction:** You can edit your information directly within the App at any time.

No data is transferred outside your device, so there are no cross-border data transfers to disclose.

---

## Your Rights Under CCPA (California Users)

If you are a California resident, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) provides you with specific rights regarding your personal information.

- **Right to know:** ShiftWell does not collect, sell, or share your personal information with third parties.
- **Right to delete:** You can delete all App data at any time through Settings > Clear All Data.
- **Right to opt out of sale:** We do not sell your personal information. There is nothing to opt out of.
- **Non-discrimination:** We will not discriminate against you for exercising any of your CCPA rights.
