3. Color Scheme Review
My honest assessment and one refinement suggestion.

Verdict: The palette is strong. One tweak.

✅ Keep As-Is

Gold accent (#C8A84B) — Perfect. Premium without being gaudy. Reads as luxury/confidence. Better than the blue accents 90% of health apps use.

Sleep purple (#7B61FF) — Psychologically aligned. Purple/violet = night, calm, sleep in user perception.

Color-coded blocks — The orange/blue/green/red system is highly scannable. Each block type is instantly recognizable.

High contrast text — White on dark navy passes WCAG AA. Good accessibility.

🔧 One Tweak

Warm the background slightly.

Current #0A0E1A is pure cold navy. For a sleep app, a tiny bit of warmth makes it feel more inviting and less like a stock trading terminal.

Suggested: #0B0D16 — barely perceptible, but subconsciously warmer. It's the difference between "clinical dark mode" and "cozy dark mode."

Surface color shifts from #141929 to #151A2A accordingly.
