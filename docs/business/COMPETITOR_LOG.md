### Competitive Positioning Intelligence

#### Timeshifter (HIGH threat, $69.99/yr) — The incumbent to displace
- **Their core claim:** Harvard-backed jet lag science for travelers and athletes.
- **Critical gap:** Manual shift entry only. No calendar integration. No recovery protocol. No dynamic rescheduling.
- **ASO presence:** Dominated by "jet lag" keywords, not "shift work." This is ours to take.
- **Price delta:** $69.99/yr vs ShiftWell $49.99/yr — we are $20/yr cheaper with more features.
- **Positioning counter:** "Timeshifter helps you recover from a flight. ShiftWell helps you live your whole life on rotation."

#### Rise Science (HIGH threat, $35.99-69.99/yr) — The beautiful app that ignores shift workers
- **Their core claim:** Sleep debt science, energy prediction, beautiful UI.
- **Critical gap:** Algorithm breaks for irregular schedules. Does not read calendars. Cannot anticipate rotation.
- **ASO presence:** "Energy," "recovery," "sleep debt" — lifestyle/performance framing.
- **Price delta:** ShiftWell is competitively priced at their lower tier, vastly better for the shift-work use case.
- **Positioning counter:** "Rise tells you how tired you are. ShiftWell tells you how to never be that tired again."

#### Arcashift ($49-69/yr) — The direct competitor that doesn't work
- **Their strength:** NIH-funded credibility, "digital twin" science branding.
- **Their weakness:** Buggy, freezes, unreliable calendar sync. Reviews confirm consistent crashes.
- **Opportunity:** Arcashift users are actively looking for a better alternative. Monitor their App Store reviews.
- **Positioning counter:** Same science. Works.
