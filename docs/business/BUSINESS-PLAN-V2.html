Executive Summary

The problem, the product, and the opportunity in one page.

The Problem

700 million shift workers globally experience chronic circadian misalignment. In the US, 32% of healthcare workers report short sleep, shift workers have 50% higher absenteeism, and nurse fatigue is a root cause in 23% of sentinel events. The annual employer cost: $136.4 billion in fatigue-related productivity loss.

The Solution

ShiftWell is an iOS app that imports a shift worker's schedule from their calendar, generates a science-backed circadian optimization plan — sleep windows, strategic naps, caffeine cutoffs, meal timing, and light protocols — and exports that plan directly back to the user's calendar. Deterministic science. Not LLM guesswork. Personalized to each worker's schedule and chronotype.

Traction

Feature-complete MVP with 618+ automated tests passing. Built by an ED physician who lives the problem daily. 28K+ lines of production code. Pre-TestFlight stage with App Store submission in preparation.

📈

Market

Global mHealth: $82.45B (2025), 22.3% CAGR. US shift workers: 22M. Global: 700M. Healthcare AI VC investment: $11.1B (2024). SAM at 7.8% conversion: $51.5M ARR.

💵

Revenue Model

B2C: $6.99/mo, $49.99/yr, $149.99 lifetime (14-day premium trial). B2B: $5–$15/seat/month for enterprise licensing. Break-even at 2 premium subscribers.

The Problem

Scale, health consequences, employer cost, and the technology gap that remains unfilled.

2.1 Scale of the Problem

Shift work is not a niche. It is the largest occupational health crisis in the developed world that nobody talks about:

700M

22M

32%
US healthcare workers with short sleep (NIOSH)

The biological mechanism is well understood. Human circadian clocks evolved over millennia for a solar day — sleep at night, wake at dawn. Night shift work forces the body to function in direct opposition to this evolved rhythm. Unlike jet lag (a temporary disruption), shift work is chronic and recurring. Most shift workers never fully adapt. They exist in a permanent state of circadian misalignment.

2.2 Health Consequences

Peer-reviewed evidence links chronic shift work to severe health outcomes:

Financial Model

A 500-nurse hospital losing 27.65% annually = 138 nurse departures. At $100K–$150K each = $13.8M–$20.7M in replacement costs annually. Sleep quality is the most prominent mediator between shift work and burnout (PMC7345885) — and burnout is the leading driver of voluntary departure.

The Solution

The closed-loop calendar system and the science that powers it.

3.1 What ShiftWell Does

ShiftWell connects your work calendar to your body clock. The app:

1. Import

Imports your shift schedule from Apple Calendar, Google Calendar, or QGenda.

2. Read

Reads your personal calendar for upcoming obligations that affect sleep timing.

3. Analyze

Analyzes your chronotype, shift pattern, and schedule context.

4. Generate

Generates a complete circadian optimization plan: sleep windows, strategic naps, caffeine cutoffs, meal timing, and light exposure recommendations.

5. Export

Exports the plan to your calendar as real, color-coded events.

6. Adapt

Adapts when your schedule changes — the loop is continuous, not one-time.
