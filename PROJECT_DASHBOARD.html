4 Science Foundation

Algorithm Modules

Core Circadian (11 modules)
circadian/sleep-windows.ts Built
THE BRAIN: Optimal sleep blocks using anchor sleep (NIOSH/CDC), Two-Process Model, gradual delay protocol. Borbely 1982, Eastman & Burgess 2009.
circadian/nap-engine.ts Built
Strategic nap placement: pre-shift 90-min cycles for nights, 25-min power naps. Milner & Cote 2009, Ruggiero & Redeker 2014.
circadian/caffeine.ts Built
Personalized cutoff: sleepOnset - (halfLife x 1.67). Drake et al. 2013 caffeine timing research.
circadian/meals.ts Built
Time-restricted eating, front-loaded calories, circadian-aligned timing. Manoogian et al. 2022, Chellappa et al. 2021.
circadian/light-protocol.ts Built
Light exposure/avoidance scheduling for phase shifting. Czeisler et al. 1990, Eastman & Burgess 2009.
circadian/energy-model.ts Built
Borbely two-process model: Process S (homeostatic pressure) + Process C (circadian pacemaker). Predicts fatigue through shift transitions.
Adaptive Brain (6 modules)
adaptive/chronotype-adapter.ts Built
Adjusts recommendations based on chronotype (morning/evening/intermediate). Horne & Ostberg 1976, individualized offsets.
adaptive/sleep-debt-tracker.ts Built
Cumulative sleep debt calculation and recovery recommendations across multi-day shift patterns. Gander et al. 2011.
adaptive/pattern-learning.ts Built
Identifies recurring shift patterns and pre-optimizes plans before shifts begin. HealthKit feedback loop ready.

6 Blockers & External Gates

Critical Path to TestFlight

1
LLC Formation (Sim Action)
File in FL (~$125, 30 min online). Required before Organization Apple Developer enrollment. Top name picks: Circadian Labs, Vigil Health.
2
D-U-N-S Number Request
Free via Apple or Dun & Bradstreet. Takes ~5 business weeks. Request immediately after LLC is filed — longest lead time on the critical path.
3
Apple Developer Enrollment
$99/yr Organization enrollment at developer.apple.com. Requires LLC EIN + D-U-N-S. Unlocks TestFlight + App Store submission.
4
Privacy Policy URL (Sim Action)
Content ready in PRIVACY_POLICY.md. Push to GitHub Pages or domain. Required in App Store listing.
