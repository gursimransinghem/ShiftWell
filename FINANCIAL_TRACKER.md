| # | Item | Cost | Status | Blocks | Notes |

| 1 | **LLC Formation** (FL) | ~$125 | NOT STARTED | Apple Developer enrollment | File via Sunbiz.org. Need: name, registered agent, articles of org |

| 3 | **Business Bank Account** | $0 | NOT STARTED | Revenue collection | Need LLC + EIN first. Chase, Mercury, or Relay recommended |
